Super Store Sales Dashboard – Power BI
Project Overview: 
This Power BI dashboard analyzes Super Store sales data from 2019–2020 to provide business insights across regions, segments, categories, and products.
The goal of this project was to practice data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard design best practices.

Key Metrics: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Average Delivery Time, Average Order Value, Profit Margin %

Dashboard Features: Interactive Region slicer, Date range filter, Monthly Sales & Profit YoY comparison, Sales breakdown by Segment, Category & Payment Mode, Top 5 Products by Sales & Profit

Tools Used: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling, Data Cleaning

Key Insights:
West region leads overall sales
Office Supplies category generates highest revenue
December is the peak sales month
While 2020 generated higher sales, profit did not increase proportionally, indicating margin fluctuations.
